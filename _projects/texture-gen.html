About

Following on from generating 3D worlds, I decided to have a look at generating textures in a small project sponsored by my University's URSS (Undergraduate Research Support Scheme).

Originally I had planned on continuing to use Perlin noise for generation, but my supervisor showed me a method of creating images by applying filters in the frequency domain to a noisy image. I wrote some software that does this, while allowing the user to control the filter's general shape.
