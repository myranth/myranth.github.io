About

The classic block-breaking game made considerably harder by being in 3D. This was one possible assignment for a computer graphics module. Although we were taught the older, simpler OpenGL, I decided to use 'modern' OpenGL i.e. version 3.3, as I had been learning that to make Lands of Terin.

Features include 4 arcade themed levels, the ability to rotate the level while you play*, markers around the edge of the level that follow the ball and some simple Blinn-Phong lighting.

*This was part of the assignment and not a design choice!
