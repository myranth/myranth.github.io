Hi there! My name is Phil Taylor. I graduated from The University of Warwick with an MEng in Computer Science in 2017. This website is primarily a portfolio to display what work I have done both academically and in my own time.

About me

I am currently a software engineer at Moju, doing a mixture of front-end and back-end work using Python/Flask, React and various AWS tools. Most of the time I use Arch Linux and Visual Studio Code for development.

I have always been interested in both playing and making games. When I was younger I designed levels for a few Flash games, then made some simple tower defence/platformer games in Game Maker. I have recently been learning more professional level technologies (C++, OpenGL, Unreal Engine, a bit of Rust as well) and am currently looking to become a programmer in the games industry. These days I mostly play DotA 2 and Overwatch, but have spent many hours of my life on Team Fortress 2, Minecraft, Runescape and Guitar Hero.

When not at a computer, I play guitar and drums. I like a good bit of power/prog rock/metal, including bands such as Dream Theater, Haken, An Endless Sporadic, Demetori. I also partake in Aikido whenever possible.
