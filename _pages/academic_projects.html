Rush Hour Solver

Python, Z3

Rush Hour is a sliding puzzle game on a 6x6 board, consisting of different sized 'cars' that can slide vertically and horizontally. The goal is to get one specific car to the exit square. The assignment was to encode the game as a propositional formula, which would then be solved by the Z3 Theorem Prover, resulting in a solution to the puzzle provided.
